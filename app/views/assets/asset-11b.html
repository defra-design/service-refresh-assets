Flood forecast for England

Ongoing river flooding is expected in parts of South Yorkshire, Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire and Lincolnshire today (Saturday) as a result of rain that has already fallen. This is expected to continue through until Wednesday in South Yorkshire, and until Sunday in Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire. Properties will flood and there will be travel disruption. Localised river and surface water flooding impacts are probable on Saturday and Sunday in parts of central and southern England and are possible more widely in parts of England and Wales. Localised river and surface water flooding impacts are also possible across parts of the southern Pennines and are possible but not expected more widely across parts of England and Wales on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday. Land, roads and some properties may flood and there may be travel disruption.
