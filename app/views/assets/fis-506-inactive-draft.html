Ginge Brook flood warning area

This is an area where we issue flood warnings. When one's in place, you need to:

- turn off the gas, water and electricity

- move your family, pets and car to safety

- move your belongings upstairs or to safety

Updated 9:55am on 30 March 2020

Flood risk area: Ginge Brook, for the Steventon area down to and including Sutton Courtenay

Get flood warnings by phone, text or email.
